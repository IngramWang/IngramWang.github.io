Biography

Currently, I am a first-year Ph.D. student at the University of Sydney under the supervision of Prof. Tongliang Liu and Prof. Dacheng Tao.

I got my master's degree in computer science in June 2021 from Nanjing University and my bachelor's degree in computer science in June 2018 from the University of Science and Technology of China respectively. After a short period of working as an algorithm engineer for the video recommendation system of TikTok, I decided to study for a Ph.D. degree to pursue an academic career.

Research Interests

My research interests include Machine Learning and Data Mining. Specifically, I am mainly focusing on the subfields:

  • Quantum Machine Learning: introducing quantum computing techniques to machine learning models to enhance their accuracy and improve their speed.
